Privacy Policy for Zombie Fast Run

Last updated: September 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled in the game Zombie Fast Run, developed and published by Andrzej Bzowski.

1. Data Collection

The game does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or geolocation data.

2. Internet Connection

The game is designed to run fully offline and does not require an internet connection. No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

No third-party SDKs, analytics, or ads are integrated into the game. User activity is not tracked in any way.

4. Local Storage

Game progress and preferences are stored locally on the user’s device and are never shared externally.

5. Children’s Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect information from children. While suitable for general audiences, it may contain simple arcade-style gameplay with mild cartoon action.

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at:

  • Email: andrzejbzowski.dev@gmail.com
  • Website: andrzejbzowskidev-dev.github.io

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
