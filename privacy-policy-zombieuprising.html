Privacy Policy for Zombie Uprising

Last updated: August 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how user data is handled in the game Zombie Uprising, developed and published by Andrzej Bzowski.

1. No Personal Data Collected

The game does not collect or store any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

2. No Internet Connection Required

The game is fully offline and does not require or establish an internet connection.

3. No Third-Party Services

Zombie Uprising does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising platforms.

4. Local-Only Data

Any game-related progress or preferences are stored locally on the user’s device and are not transmitted anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect any data from children. It is suitable for general audiences but may include content with cartoon violence.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the game, feel free to contact the developer:

  • Email: andrzejbzowski.dev@gmail.com
  • Developer Website: andrzejbzowskidev-dev.github.io

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
